Dorayaki Recipe🍡

Ingredients

For the batter:

  • 2 eggs
  • 1/2 cup(100g) sugar
  • 1 tpsp honey
  • 1 cup(120g)all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 tsp baking powder
  • 2-3 tbsp water(to adjust batter consistency)

For the filling:

  • 1/2 to cup anko(sweet red bean paste)(you can use store-bought or homemade)

Instructions

  1. In a bowl, whisk the eggs, sugar, and honey until fluffy and pale.
  2. Sift in the flour and baking powder, and gently mix.
  3. Let the batter rest for 10–15 minutes. Then add a tablespoon of water at a time until the batter becomes smooth and slightly runny.
  4. Heat a non-stick pan on medium-low heat. Lightly oil and wipe off excess
  5. Pour about 2 tbsp of batter to make small round pancakes (like mini pancakes).
  6. Cook until bubbles appear on the surface (1.5–2 minutes), then flip and cook for 30–60 seconds more.
